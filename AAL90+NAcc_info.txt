This node list contains a subset of nodes from the AAL plus the bilateral Nucleus Accumbens.

The subset of nodes from the Automated Anatomical Labelling atlas includes cortical and subcortical regions, excluding the cerebellar regions. (the first 90 AAL regions)

This list was designed for the music study for Carina Freitas.
